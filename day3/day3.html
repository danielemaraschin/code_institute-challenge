How to make Brigadeiro

image

At the very least, 'Brigadeiro' is a very sweet and the most famous candy from Brazil. There is not a person in the world who does not love this magnificent candy as soon as he tastes it for the first time.

Ingredients:

  • Condensend Milk;
  • 2 Tbsp Power Chocolate;
  • 1 Tbsp Butter;
  • 1 cup Chocolate Sprinkles.

Preparation

  1. In a non-stick pan, melt the butter on low flame;
  2. Add the others ingredients into the pan;
  3. Stir well with a silicon-spatula until the mixture thickened;
  4. The mixture should hold together and be slightly sticky;
  5. Set aside until it is coold
  6. With your hands, make small balls with the dough and roll them into the Chocolate Sprinkles.
